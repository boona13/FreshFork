Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 28, 2025

Welcome to FreshFork! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how FreshFork ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Recipe Requests:

We process your recipe requests using AI to generate personalized meal suggestions. Your requests are sent to our AI service provider to generate responses.

Saved Recipes:

Recipes you save are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your saved recipes to our servers.

Subscription Information:

When you subscribe, Apple processes your payment information. We receive anonymized transaction data from Apple to verify your subscription status.

Language Preference:

Your selected language is stored locally on your device to provide content in your preferred language.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Generate personalized recipe recommendations
  • Provide AI-generated food images
  • Verify your subscription status
  • Improve our app and services
  • Provide multi-language support

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal information
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts or photos

3. Data Storage

Local Storage:

All your saved recipes, generated images, and preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

AI Processing:

Your recipe requests are temporarily processed by our AI service provider (Google Gemini via OpenRouter) to generate responses. These requests are not permanently stored.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Apple App Store:

Handles subscription payments and transaction processing.

OpenRouter/Google Gemini:

Processes your recipe requests to generate AI-powered responses and images.

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of information.

5. Nutritional Information

AI-Generated Estimates:

All nutritional information provided by FreshFork is AI-generated and based on established nutritional databases including USDA FoodData Central, FDA guidelines, and WHO nutritional standards. These values are estimates and not based on laboratory analysis.

Data Sources:

Our AI references standardized nutritional data from reputable sources to calculate approximate nutritional values. Actual nutritional content may vary based on ingredient brands, preparation methods, and portion sizes.

Disclaimer:

Nutritional information is provided for informational purposes only. If you have specific dietary requirements, allergies, or medical conditions, please consult with healthcare professionals or registered dietitians. FreshFork is not a medical application and should not be used as a substitute for professional nutritional advice.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data stored locally on your device is protected by iOS security
  • Communications with AI services use encrypted connections
  • We do not store your personal information on our servers

7. Children's Privacy

FreshFork is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all saved recipes from your device
  • Cancel your subscription at any time
  • Change your language preferences
  • Request deletion of any data we may have

To delete all app data, you can delete the app from your device or use the iOS Settings to clear app data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
